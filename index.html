Lynnelle Ye

Hello! As of spring 2020, I am an NSF postdoctoral fellow in the Stanford math department, sponsored by Richard Taylor.

I got my Ph.D. from Harvard in 2019, working with Mark Kisin. My thesis was on slopes in eigenvarieties for definite unitary groups.

I got my B.S. in mathematics with honors from Stanford in 2014, with a minor in computer science.

I can be contacted at lynnelle at stanford dot edu.

Research

I work in algebraic number theory. Currently, my area of focus is p-adic automorphic forms, specifically eigenvarieties.
